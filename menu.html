Menu

Goan dishes fit for all kings. Very simplistic food packing in flavor is the defining the corner stone of Indian cuisine from state to state. Food is informed by religion, population, and geography, though interpretations on the best way to cook something varies wildly even between friendly neighbors. You won't find dishes from Goa any where else in India, it's simply the best!
